## Malicious Actors

A person, machine or entity for an event or incident that impacts or has the potential to impact the normal functioning of another entity.


## Inventory

## Attack Vectors

## Attack Surface
The sum of the [Attack Vectors](#Attack%20Vectors)

## Security Controls
